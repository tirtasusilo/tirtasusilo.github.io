Tirta Susilo

Tirta is a lecturer in the School of Psychology at Victoria University of Wellington. He is a member of the Cognitive and Behavioural Neuroscience (CBNS) group. As an undergraduate Tirta studied industrial engineering at Parahyangan University and psychology at the University of Queensland. He received his PhD in cognitive psychology from the Australian National University and was a postdoctoral associate in cognitive neuroscience at Dartmouth College. Tirta is originally from Jakarta, Indonesia.
